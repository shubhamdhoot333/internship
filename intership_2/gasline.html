Risk of Poor Installation

Gas Leaks are a major problem that can put you, your employees, and your entire business at risk. Those exposed to a gas leak can become very sick, very quickly. A leak can even result in an explosion under the right circumstances. With this in mind, it’s important to have trained and certified professionals perform work on your gas line and plumbing. Shut off gas valves if possible, evacuate the building, and avoid flames or turning on any appliances. It’s odorless and tasteless, so you won’t be able to detect a leak if you don’t have the proper emergency detectors in place. Carbon monoxide poisoning can be deadly, so it’s essential to have your plumbing and detectors properly installed and maintained.
